About Us

Holistic Counselling and Training Centre was founded in 2021 by two professional Counselling Psychologists with a passion, commitment and goal to strive for a mentally fit community and a vision to make mental health facilities and trainings easily accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To promote mental health care accessible in the society and to promote community participation and to stimulate efforts towards self help in the community.

Affiliated with InSPA (Indian School Psychology Association)
Registered under Society Registration Act, 1860
Supported by CCi and Career Nakhsha

Our Services

Individual Counselling

For issues including self-esteem, depression, anxiety, stress, loneliness, grief, job loss, sexual orientation, sexuality, chronic illness and sexual abuse.

Couple Counselling

For those who want to improve communication, problem solving, conflict management, and sexual issues.

Family Counselling

For dealing with the loss of a family member, conflict, effective discipline, substance abuse, school problems, parenting issues and concerns of aging parents.

Career Counselling

Professional guidance to help you make informed career decisions and navigate your professional journey.

Areni Tungoe

Certified Registered Counsellor

Areni Tungoe is a certified and registered Counsellor under the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India (Reg. No. 114k72-501156622324). Holding a Master of Arts in Pastoral Counselling from the Asian Theological Seminary in Manila, Philippines, and a Master of Divinity from Southern Asia Bible College, Bangalore, Karnataka, she is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Counselling Psychology with a focus on Behavioural Science. With a passion for making a difference in people's lives, her work spans across NGOs, schools, and colleges both in India and abroad, offering rich cross-cultural experience in counselling and teaching. Her training includes certificate courses such as COVID-19 Psychological First Aid from Public Health England, Career Counseling and Guidance from the Counsellor Council of India, and Integrating Spirituality in Christian Counselling from Indonesia. Professionally, she is affiliated with several organizations: a registered member of the All Nagaland Counsellor Association (ANCA), Board Member of the Christian Counsellor Association of India (CCAI), and State Convenor for the Indian School of Psychology Association (InSPA). She was also a member of the Philippines Association of Christian Counsellors (PACC). In 2021, her contributions were recognized with the Prof. Erika Voigt Young Scientist Award in School Psychology. Currently serving full-time as a Children’s Director at Kohima Lotha Assembly of God Church, she is also a dedicated research scholar and the proprietor of the Holistic Counselling and Training Centre.
